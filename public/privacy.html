Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FreshZone ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our campus vape aerosol detection system.

FreshZone is a real-time air quality monitoring system designed for campus environments. We collect sensor data and user information to provide alerts and maintain system security.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name: First name and last name
  • Email Address: Used for account verification and alerts
  • Phone Number: Used for two-factor authentication and emergency notifications
  • Employee ID: For identity verification within the campus system
  • Position: Administrator or Staff/Teacher role
  • Password: Stored in encrypted form (bcrypt hash)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • IP Address: For security and rate limiting
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Login Attempts: Timestamp, IP address, success/failure status
  • Cookies: Session cookies and preference cookies (see Cookie Policy below)

2.3 Sensor Data

  • PM1.0, PM2.5, PM10 readings: Air quality measurements from IoT sensors
  • AQI values: Calculated air quality index
  • Detection events: Timestamps and locations of vape aerosol detections
  • Sensor status: Online/offline status, last seen timestamps

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Creating and managing your user account
  • Authentication: Verifying your identity and securing access
  • Real-time Alerts: Sending notifications when vape aerosol is detected
  • System Security: Preventing unauthorized access and detecting fraud
  • Analytics: Understanding usage patterns to improve the system
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory obligations
  • Communication: Responding to your inquiries and support requests

4. Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Data Type Retention Period Reason
User Accounts Until account deletion + 30 days Account recovery, audit purposes
Login Attempts 24 hours Rate limiting, security monitoring
Sensor Readings (PM values) 90 days Historical analysis, trend monitoring
Detection Events 1 year Compliance, incident reporting
Email Logs 30 days Delivery verification, troubleshooting
Push Notification Logs 30 days Delivery verification, troubleshooting
OTP Codes Until expiration (15 minutes) Security, one-time use only

Automatic Deletion: Our system automatically purges expired data on a scheduled basis using cron jobs. You may also request immediate deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting our Data Protection Officer.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who assist in operating our platform (e.g., hosting providers, email services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of FreshZone, our users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly agree to share your information

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute-force attacks
  • CSRF Protection: Cross-site request forgery tokens for all state-changing requests
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions (Administrator vs Staff)
  • Database Security: MySQL with SSL connections and prepared statements
  • Regular Audits: Security monitoring and logging of system access

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Right to Restriction: Request limitation of data processing
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer using the contact information below.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication and security (session cookies)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings (e.g., dark mode preference)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use the platform (if analytics are enabled)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of the platform.

Cookie Consent: When you first visit FreshZone, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. You may accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your preference will be stored and can be changed at any time.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions, to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Children's Privacy

FreshZone is designed for use in educational and campus environments. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16 years of age. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our platform or sending you an email. Your continued use of FreshZone after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
