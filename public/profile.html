Managing Your Account & Notifications

Account Settings
Profile photoYour photo is displayed on detection logs and audit exports. Upload a clear, current photo so your identity is recognizable in incident documentation.
Display nameYour display name appears on every event you acknowledge or resolve. Keep it consistent with how you are identified in school records so audit trails are correctly attributable.
Notification settingsPush notifications are delivered to devices where you have enabled them. Use the bell icon in the top navigation to toggle alerts on your current device. Multiple devices can be registered at once.

Your role and permissions

Your account role determines available actions. Staff can respond to and resolve alerts. Administrators can also acknowledge alerts, manage accounts, and delete detection history records.

Your name on audit records

Every alert you acknowledge or resolve is permanently logged with your name, role, and the exact timestamp. These records are exportable as CSV for use in formal school incident reports.

Account security

Use the change password option to keep your credentials secure. Do not share your login — each session is tied to your account and recorded in the system log for accountability.
